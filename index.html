Popular destinations in Japan

Sensō-ji is an ancient Buddhist temple in the Asakusadistrict of Tokyo. It is one of the most popular temples in Japan, and is known for its large red gate, Nakamise Shopping Street, and Asakusa Shrine Visitors to Sensō-ji can explore the temple grounds, which include several buildings, a five-story pagoda, and a large garden. Visitors can also purchase souvenirs from the Nakamise Shopping Street, which is lined with shops selling traditional Japanese goods.

Kaiyukan Aquarium: This world-class aquarium is home to over 30,000 marine animals from all over the world. Visitors can see everything from whale sharks and manta rays to penguins and sea turtles.

Kinkaku-ji Temple (金閣寺): This iconic temple is also known as the Golden Pavilion, and it is one of the most visited tourist attractions in Kyoto. The temple is covered in gold leaf, and it sits on a beautiful lake.
